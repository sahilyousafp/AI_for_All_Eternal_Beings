A painterly oil painting of a cross-section of living soil, viewed from the side.
The style is Studio Ghibli meets Björk Biophilia — soft organic brushstrokes,
warm golden rim lighting, visible brush texture, slightly glowing root hairs,
dreamlike but not cartoonish. The palette is rich dark earth browns, warm
honey highlights, with subtle cool turquoise accents in the deepest shadows.

Composition: centered vertical soil column, ground surface at the top third,
deep roots and mycelium threading through the lower two thirds. A sense of
quiet reverence. As if painted by a naturalist who also dreams in color.

Mood: The ground is alive. You can almost hear it breathing.

No text. No people. No buildings. No modern objects. No photorealism.
No digital art aesthetic. No generic AI art. Painterly only.

Square aspect ratio, 1024x1024.
