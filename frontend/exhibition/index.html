The Living Layer microbial indicators

The columns above show carbon. This panel shows the soil's living community — the microbes that decide whether carbon stays or leaves. They respond in seasons, not decades, so they are the early-warning system for everything else. A teaspoon of healthy soil contains more living organisms than there are humans on Earth.
